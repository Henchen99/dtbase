About DesignandTechBase

Welcome to DesignandTechBase, your ultimate free resource hub for GCSE and A-Level Design & Technology students. Our mission is to provide high-quality revision materials, practice exercises, and coursework examples to help students excel in their exams.

Our Mission

At DTBase, we believe that education should be accessible to all. Our goal is to support students with comprehensive and up-to-date materials, ensuring they have everything they need to succeed.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✅ High-quality revision notes tailored for GCSE & A-Level students
  • ✅ Free access to past papers and coursework examples
  • ✅ A growing library of exercises and questions
  • ✅ Expert tutoring services for personalised learning
  • ✅ A supportive community of learners
