Materials and objects can be manipulated to resist and work with forces and stresses


Force Definition Examples
Tension
  • Force that pulls materials apart
  • Stretches the object along its length
  • Steel cables in suspension bridges
  • Rubber bands being stretched
Compression
  • Force that squeezes materials together
  • Reduces length or volume
  • Concrete columns in buildings
  • Springs in car suspensions
Bending
  • Force that deforms material into a curve
  • Combination of tension and compression
  • Bookshelves under load
  • Beams in bridges
Torsion
  • Twisting force causing rotation
  • Creates shear stress within the material
  • Drive shafts in vehicles
  • Turning a screwdriver
Shear
  • Forces act in opposite directions, parallel to surface
  • Causes material to slide along a plane
  • Scissors cutting paper
  • Bolts in machinery under load
