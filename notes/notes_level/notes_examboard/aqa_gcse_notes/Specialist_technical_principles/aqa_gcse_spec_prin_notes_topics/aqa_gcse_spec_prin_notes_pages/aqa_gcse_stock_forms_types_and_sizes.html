Papers and Boards:

Detailed Information:

Cartridge paper: Medium weight paper (120-150gsm) with slight texture, ideal for sketching and drawing.

Corrugated card: Fluted inner layer between flat liners, providing excellent protection and cushioning (used for packaging).

Sheet forms: Standard paper sizes follow ISO 216 system (A0=841×1189mm, A1=594×841mm etc.), with each size being half the previous.

Ply: Refers to layers - e.g., 3-ply board has three bonded layers for extra strength.

Weight measurement: Grams per square meter (gsm) indicates thickness - e.g., 80gsm=standard printer paper, 300gsm=heavy card.

Standard components: Includes treasury tags for document binding, ring binders, bulldog clips, and self-adhesive seals.

Detailed Information:

Softwoods: Typically pine, spruce or cedar. Sold as PAR (planed all round) or rough sawn. Common sizes: 100×50mm, 150×25mm etc.

Hardwoods: Including oak, beech and mahogany. Often sold by cubic foot rather than linear dimensions due to higher value.

Manufactured boards:

  • MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard) - 3mm to 25mm thicknesses
  • Plywood - typically 4'×8' sheets in 3mm to 25mm thicknesses
  • Chipboard - often 18mm thick for flat-pack furniture

Standard moldings: Includes skirting boards (typically 70-120mm height), architraves (45-70mm width), and dowels (6mm to 25mm diameter).

KD fittings: Knock-down fittings like cam locks, connector bolts and corner blocks used in flat-pack furniture assembly.

Detailed Information:

Ferrous metals: Mild steel typically sold in 1m, 2m or 6m lengths. Sheet metal commonly 0.7mm to 3mm thick.

Non-ferrous: Aluminium often in 3m lengths, copper pipes in 15mm to 28mm diameters for plumbing.

Alloys: Brass rods commonly 5mm to 25mm diameter, stainless steel sheet in 0.5mm to 3mm thicknesses.

Standard sizes:

  • Sheet: 1200×2400mm common for steel
  • Round bar: 6mm to 50mm diameter
  • Square tube: 20×20mm up to 50×50mm

Fasteners:

  • Machine screws: M3 to M12 (metric thread sizes)
  • Nuts: Match screw sizes with standard (ISO) or nyloc variants
  • Pop rivets: 3mm to 6mm diameter in aluminium or steel

    • Length
    • Width
    • Gauge
    • Diameter

Detailed Information:

Thermoplastics:

  • Acrylic (PMMA): Sheets typically 1mm to 25mm thick, rods 5mm to 50mm diameter
  • ABS: Often sold as granules for injection molding or 3D printer filament (1.75mm/2.85mm)
  • Polypropylene: Film from 20μm to 200μm thickness

Thermosets:

  • Epoxy resins: Sold as two-part kits (resin + hardener)
  • Melamine: Laminates typically 0.5mm to 1.5mm thick

Foams:

  • Polystyrene: Sheet insulation 25mm to 100mm thick
  • Polyurethane: Flexible foam in 10mm to 100mm sheets

Measurement: Gauge refers to thickness (1 gauge = 0.01mm), so 100 gauge = 1mm thick plastic sheet.

Specialist fasteners: Includes plastic rivets, threaded inserts for plastics, and nylon washers.

Detailed Information:

Natural fibers:

  • Cotton: Typically 112cm to 150cm wide, sold by meter
  • Wool: Yarn weights from 2ply (fine) to 12ply (chunky)

Synthetic fibers:

  • Polyester: Common fabric width 150cm
  • Nylon: Measured in denier (40D for sheer tights up to 1000D for heavy bags)

Fabric weights:

  • Lightweight: 30-150 gsm (voile, chiffon)
  • Medium: 150-350 gsm (cotton poplin)
  • Heavy: 350+ gsm (denim, canvas)

Standard components:

  • Zips: Sized by teeth width (e.g., #3=3mm, #5=5mm)
  • Press studs: 7mm to 15mm diameter
  • Velcro: Available in 20mm to 100mm widths

Types Sizes/Specifications Standard Components

    • Volt rating
    • Current rating
    • Resistance (ohms)
  • E12 resistor series
  • Dual in-line IC packages (DIL)

Detailed Information:

Resistors:

  • E12 series: 10, 12, 15, 18, 22, 27, 33, 39, 47, 56, 68, 82 (and multiples of 10)
  • Common power ratings: 0.25W, 0.5W, 1W
  • Tolerance: Typically ±5% for E12 series

Capacitors:

  • Electrolytic: 1μF to 4700μF, voltage ratings from 6.3V to 100V
  • Ceramic: 1pF to 1μF, small 0805 or 1206 surface mount sizes

DIL packages:

  • Common pin counts: 8, 14, 16, 18, 20, 28, 40
  • Standard spacing: 2.54mm (0.1") between pins

Microcontrollers:

  • PIC series: PIC10F to PIC32MX with 6 to 100 pins
  • Voltage: Typically 3.3V or 5V operation
  • Clock speed: 4MHz to 200MHz depending on model

Transistors:

  • BC548 (NPN) and BC558 (PNP) common for general purpose
  • Maximum ratings: 30V, 100mA typically
