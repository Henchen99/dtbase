The use of production aids

Measurement/Reference Points:

  • Fixed points on a workpiece or workspace used to ensure consistent measuring, marking, and positioning

Templates:

  • A template is used to guide the marking out of shapes on materials

Advantages Disadvantages
  • Speeds up marking out process
  • Ensures consistency across multiple pieces
  • Easy to use and low cost to make
  • Not suitable for guiding tools – only marking
  • Can wear out or become damaged over time
  • Less accurate than jigs for detailed work

Jigs:

  • A jig is used to control the motion of a tool

Advantages Disadvantages
  • Increase rate of production
  • Allow for repeatable accuracy
  • Requires little skill to use
  • Jig must be bought/created before it can be used
  • Only suitable for low volume/batch production

Patterns:

  • A pattern is used to create a mould cavity in sand casting

Advantages Disadvantages
  • Can be reused multiple times
  • Enables consistent and accurate moulds
  • Useful for complex shapes
  • Requires time and effort to make the pattern
  • Pattern wear can affect accuracy over time
  • More suitable for batch than mass production
