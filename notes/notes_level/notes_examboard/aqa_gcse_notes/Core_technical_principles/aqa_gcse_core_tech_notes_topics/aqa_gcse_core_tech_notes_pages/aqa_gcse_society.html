Design for Disabilities:

  • Mobility: OXO Good Grips kitchen tools (arthritis-friendly)
  • Vision: Microsoft Xbox Adaptive Controller (tactile interfaces)
  • Hearing: Google Live Caption (real-time audio transcription)
  • Cognitive: IKEA pictorial assembly instructions

Design for Elderly:

  • Safety: Walk-in bathtubs (Kohler) with non-slip surfaces
  • Usability: Jitterbug phones (simplified interfaces)
  • Health: Philips medication dispensers with alarms
  • Social: GrandPad tablet (curated communication)

Design for Religious Groups:

  • Islamic Needs: Modest activewear (Nike Pro Hijab)
  • Jewish Needs: Kosher-certified appliances (separate meat/dairy modes)
  • Hindu Needs: Vegetarian food packaging (green dot labeling)
  • Universal: Airport prayer rooms with qibla compasses

Design Principles:

  • Permanent: Wheelchair ramps
  • Temporary: Pregnancy seatbelts
  • Situational: Voice assistants for multitaskers
