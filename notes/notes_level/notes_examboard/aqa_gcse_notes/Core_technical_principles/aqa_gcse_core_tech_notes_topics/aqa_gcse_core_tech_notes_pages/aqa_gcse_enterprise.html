Enterprise & Business Innovations:

Crowd Funding:

  • Raising small amounts of money from many people via online platforms
  • Used by startups to launch products without traditional investors

Advantages Disadvantages
  • Access to capital without giving up equity
  • Validates market demand before production
  • Builds customer community
  • Platform fees (5-12% of funds raised)
  • Requires strong marketing effort
  • Failed projects damage reputation

Examples: Kickstarter, Indiegogo

Virtual Marketing & Retail:

  • Selling directly to consumers through digital channels
  • Uses social media, influencers and online marketplaces

Advantages Disadvantages
  • Lower overhead than physical stores
  • Global customer reach
  • Real-time sales data analytics
  • High competition for visibility
  • Dependent on platform algorithms
  • Returns/logistics challenges

Examples: Shopify stores, Instagram shops

Co-operatives:

  • Businesses owned and run by members (workers or customers)
  • Profits shared based on participation

Advantages Disadvantages
  • Democratic decision making
  • Strong employee motivation
  • Community reinvestment
  • Slower decision processes
  • Capital raising challenges
  • Potential skill gaps in members

Examples: The Co-op, Mondragon Corporation

Fair Trade:

  • Ethical certification ensuring fair wages/sustainable practices
  • Premium prices support producer communities

Advantages Disadvantages
  • Improved producer livelihoods
  • Premium market positioning
  • Environmental sustainability
  • Higher product costs
  • Complex certification process
  • Limited consumer awareness

Examples: Fairtrade coffee, Divine Chocolate
