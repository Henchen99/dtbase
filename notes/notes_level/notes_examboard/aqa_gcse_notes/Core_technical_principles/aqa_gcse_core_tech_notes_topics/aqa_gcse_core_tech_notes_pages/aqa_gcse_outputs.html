Outputs

Output Devices in Product Design:

  • Buzzers: Microwave end-of-cycle alerts (frequency modulation + sound waves)
  • Speakers: Smart home voice assistants (audio signal processing + Fourier transforms)
  • Lamps: Traffic signal systems (LED power efficiency + Ohm's Law)
  • Combined Systems: Emergency alarms (synchronized light/sound patterns)

Design Principles:

  • Permanent: Fire alarm systems (continuous monitoring)
  • Temporary: Timer progress indicators (visual/audio countdowns)
  • Situational: Accessibility cues (tactile paving with audible signals)
