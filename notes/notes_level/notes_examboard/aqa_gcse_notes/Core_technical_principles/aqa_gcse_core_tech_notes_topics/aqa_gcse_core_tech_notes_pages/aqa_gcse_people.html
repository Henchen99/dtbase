Technology Push vs Market Pull:

  • Technology Push: Innovations driving new markets (e.g., iPhone creating smartphone demand)
  • Market Pull: Consumer needs pulling tech development (e.g., renewable energy demand driving battery research)
  • Hybrid Example: COVID-19 vaccine development (pushed by mRNA tech, pulled by pandemic needs)
  • Data-Driven Choices: Amazon using purchase history to guide warehouse robotics development

Changing Job Roles:

  • Emerging Roles: AI trainers, drone operators, 3D printing technicians
  • Transformed Roles: Bank tellers → financial advisors (due to ATMs/online banking)
  • Displaced Roles: Assembly line workers replaced by collaborative robots (cobots)
  • Upskilling Examples: Automotive technicians training in EV battery systems

Case Studies:

  • Healthcare: Surgical nurses adapting to robotic surgery systems like da Vinci
  • Retail: Cashiers transitioning to "customer experience specialists" with self-checkout tech
  • Construction: Traditional architects learning BIM (Building Information Modeling)
