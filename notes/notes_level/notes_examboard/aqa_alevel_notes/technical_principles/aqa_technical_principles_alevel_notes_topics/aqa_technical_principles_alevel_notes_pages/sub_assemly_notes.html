Sub-assembly

Sub-Assembly Production:

  • A manufacturing method where components are pre-assembled separately before final product integration
  • Different production lines work simultaneously on various sub-sections of a product
  • Enables specialization and parallel production to increase efficiency

Key Knowledge:

  • Understand how sub-assembly reduces final assembly time
  • Explain why different specialist teams may work on different sub-assemblies
  • Recognize examples where standardised sub-assemblies are used across multiple products

Examples: Car engines (pre-assembled then installed), computer motherboards, modular furniture components
