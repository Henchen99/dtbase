    Case Study: Smartphone Evolution

    Phase Design Changes Market Impact
    Introduction (2007) Touchscreen interface Created new product category
    Growth (2010-2015) Larger screens, 4G support Mass market adoption
    Maturity (Present) Incremental feature updates Longer replacement cycles

    Designer's Role: Continuously adapt products through life cycle stages to maintain relevance, profitability and sustainability.
