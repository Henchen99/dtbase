Influence of aesthetics, ergonomics and anthropometrics on design, development and manufacture of products

Form over function:

  • Aesthetics over functionality
  • Aethetics as prime diver
  • Supporters advocate the aesthetics of products contributing towards an overall aethetically pleasing environment
  • Form is the main focus of the design

Uses: Juicy Salif, Hot Bertaa kettle

Form follows function:

  • Functionality over aethetics
  • Functionality as prime driver
  • product should be fit for purpose without any unnecessary decoration

Uses: F1 cars, wind turbines, racing bikes
